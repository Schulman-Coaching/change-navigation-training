You have heard about AI. You have probably used it without realizing. You have questions. This workshop answers them — not with technical language, but with clear explanations and hands-on practice.

## Who This Is For

Women who want to understand AI but have felt shut out by the conversation. You do not need to be "technical." You do not need to have used ChatGPT before. You need curiosity and a willingness to try.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: What AI Actually Is**
Not magic. Not a brain. A tool that recognizes patterns. We demystify the language — machine learning, large language models, generative AI — in plain English. You will open your first AI tool and just look at it. No pressure. No goals.

**Session 2: How to Talk to AI**
Prompting is not programming. It is conversation. You will learn the skill of asking clear questions and refining your requests. By the end, you will have used AI to help with a real task from your life.

**Session 3: What AI Can and Cannot Do**
AI is powerful. It also hallucinates, confabulates, and gets things wrong. You will learn to assess what AI produces — when to trust it, when to verify, and when to walk away.

**Session 4: AI in Your Daily Life**
Practical applications for frum women: meal planning, lesson preparation, email writing, research for learning, managing household information. You will leave with a personal list of ways AI can serve your actual life.

A laptop or tablet with internet access. That is it.
