AI is changing how work gets done. Not someday. Now. This workshop is for women who want to use AI strategically — to work more effectively, grow a business, or prepare for what is coming in their industry.

## Who This Is For

Women who are comfortable with basic technology and want to integrate AI into their professional life. You might be running a business, working in an organization, or looking ahead to career opportunities. Some familiarity with AI tools (ChatGPT or similar) is helpful but not required.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: AI for Productivity**
Automating repetitive tasks, summarizing information, managing schedules, processing documents. The goal is not to work faster. It is to free up attention for what only you can do.

**Session 2: AI for Client & Customer Communication**
Drafting emails, proposals, newsletters, and responses. Maintaining your voice while increasing your output. When to use AI. When to write yourself.

**Session 3: AI for Marketing & Content**
Generating ideas, writing copy, creating social media content, analyzing what works. For business owners and professionals who need to communicate — and do not have a marketing team.

**Session 4: AI for Operations & Systems**
Using AI for data analysis, project management, research, and process improvement. Practical tools you can implement the same week.

**Session 5: AI Strategy for Your Specific Context**
Every industry is being affected differently. You will assess what AI means for your particular field — and map a practical integration plan for the next 90 days.

**Session 6: Staying Current Without Burning Out**
The AI landscape changes weekly. You will learn how to stay informed without feeling overwhelmed — filtering signal from noise, identifying what actually matters for your work.

## Prerequisites

Basic comfort with a computer and an AI tool such as ChatGPT. If you have not used AI before, we recommend starting with AI Fundamentals for Everyday Life first.
