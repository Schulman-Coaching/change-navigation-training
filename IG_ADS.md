# Instagram Ad Creative — Change Navigation Training

## Campaign Structure

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Objective** | Traffic → Lead magnet landing page |
| **Budget** | $100–500/mo test budget |
| **Placement** | Instagram Feed + Stories (Facebook Feed optional) |
| **Schedule** | Always on, pause to review weekly |
| **Bid strategy** | Lowest cost (start), switch to cost cap after data |

## Audience Targeting

### Primary Audience (Broad — Let Meta Optimize)
- **Location**: United States (or NY/NJ metro for tighter targeting)
- **Gender**: Women
- **Age**: 25–55
- **Languages**: English
- **Detailed targeting**: Orthodox Judaism, Jewish (religious), or leave open — let Meta find them via behavior
- **Exclude**: Existing email subscribers (upload customer list)

### Interest-Based Test Audiences
| Audience Name | Interests |
|---|---|
| Career Changers | Career development, Job hunting, Professional certification, LinkedIn, Online learning |
| Busy Moms | Parenting, Time management, Working mother, Meal planning, Household management |
| Lifelong Learners | Online learning, Coursera, Skillshare, Self-improvement, Personal development |
| Jewish Community | Orthodox Judaism, Jewish holidays, Kosher food, Jewish education, Jewish parenting |

## Budget Allocation (Test Month)

| Variant | Audience | Daily Budget | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| A (Reframe) | Broad 25–55 | $10/day | 7 days |
| B (Time) | Busy Moms | $10/day | 7 days |
| C (Belonging) | Career Changers | $10/day | 7 days |
| **Total test** | | **$210** | **7 days** |

After 7 days: kill underperformers (CTR <1%, CPL >$5). Scale winners at 20% per week.

## Retargeting (Month 2+)

| Audience | Ad |
|---|---|
| Assessment completers (7 days) | "Ready for the next step? Book a free call." → Calendly |
| Blog readers (14 days) | Free workshop story → assessment page |
| Workshop attendees | Membership offer → memberships page |

## Key Metrics to Watch

| Metric | Healthy | Needs Work |
|---|---|---|
| CTR (click-through rate) | >1.5% | <0.8% |
| CPL (cost per landing page view) | <$2 | >$5 |
| Assessment completion rate | >40% | <25% |
| Email signup from assessment | Set up tracking first | — |
| Free call booked from email | Track manually for now | — |
