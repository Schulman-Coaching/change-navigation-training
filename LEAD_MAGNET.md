# Lead Magnet — Change Readiness Self-Assessment

A 10-question PDF. 3 minutes. Four result profiles, each linked to the right CNT program.

---

## The 10 Questions (1 = Not at all → 5 = Exactly)

1. When a new technology appears, my first instinct is to learn about it.
2. I feel confident that I can adapt to changes in my work or industry.
3. I have a clear sense of what I want my next chapter to look like.
4. When I face a difficult decision, I trust my ability to make it — even with incomplete information.
5. I have a community of women I can talk to about navigating change.
6. The pace of change in the world feels manageable to me.
7. I actively seek out opportunities to learn new skills.
8. I have a system or framework for handling uncertainty.
9. I feel like change is something that happens *to* me. (Reverse-scored: 1→5, 2→4, 3→3, 4→2, 5→1)
10. I am excited about what AI can do for my life — not afraid of it.
