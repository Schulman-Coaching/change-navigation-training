## Channel Mix

### Organic (Free)

| Channel | Tactic | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| **Blog (SEO)** | CNT-voice posts. Target: "AI for frum women," "career change Orthodox," "managing overwhelm." | 1–2/week |
| **WhatsApp groups** | Join frum women's groups. Share posts. Offer free calls. No hard sell. | 2–3x/week |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional positioning. Change navigation + AI articles. | 1/week |
| **Speaking** | Community events, school parent nights, shul programs. | 1–2/month |

### Paid (When Revenue Supports)

| Channel | Tactic | Budget |
|---|---|---|
| **Instagram/Facebook ads** | Orthodox Jewish women, NY/NJ, 25–55, interests in career/parenting/education. | $100–500/mo |
| **Frum media** | Mishpacha, Ami, Jewish publications. Announce new cohorts. | $200–500/placement |
| **Referral program** | "$50 credit for each member you bring." | Discount cost |
