You are running a household, raising children, and possibly working. The mental load is real. This workshop is about handing some of it off — to a tool that never sleeps, never complains, and never forgets.

## Who This Is For

Women 25-35 managing households with young children. You do not need AI experience. You need the desire to reclaim some of your evening.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: Meal Planning and Household Organization**
AI-powered meal plans, grocery lists, and weekly schedules. Turn "what's for dinner" from a daily crisis into a solved problem.

**Session 2: Family Communication and Scheduling**
School emails, activity coordination, family updates. AI drafts. You personalize. Hours recovered.

**Session 3: Personal Productivity With AI**
Managing your own tasks, goals, and projects — whether professional, personal, or both. AI as your executive assistant.

**Session 4: Building Systems That Last**
Templates, automations, and repeatable workflows. Leave with a personal system you can maintain in ten minutes a day.
