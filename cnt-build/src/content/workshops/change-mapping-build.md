Your twenties and thirties are a cascade of transitions. Marriage. First child. Maybe a second. Career changes. Moves. Each one arrives with its own disorientation. This workshop gives you a framework for navigating any transition with clarity.

## Who This Is For

Women in the thick of life transitions — new marriage, new parenthood, career shifts, relocation. If you feel like you are constantly adjusting and never quite catching up, this is for you.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: Mapping the Transition**
Separate three things that blur together: the external change, your internal response, and the choices available. A framework that works for any transition.

**Session 2: From Map to Movement**
Build a personal transition plan with concrete next steps, support structures, and a timeline that respects your pace and your real life.
