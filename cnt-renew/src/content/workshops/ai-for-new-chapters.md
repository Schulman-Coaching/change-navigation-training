You are entering a new chapter. Maybe the children have left. Maybe you are considering a career change. Maybe you are looking at retirement and wondering what comes next. AI can be the tool that helps you write that chapter — on your terms.

## Who This Is For

Women 50+ navigating a life transition. No AI experience needed. No technical background required. Patience, curiosity, and a willingness to learn at your own pace.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: What AI Is — In Plain Language**
Demystified. No jargon. What these tools actually do, how they work in simple terms, and why they matter for someone at your stage of life.

**Session 2: AI for Everyday Life**
Managing information, planning projects, researching topics, organizing personal records, writing correspondence. Practical uses you will reach for immediately.

**Session 3: AI for Your Next Move**
Whether it is a new career, a volunteer role, a business idea, or a personal project — using AI to research, plan, and execute.

**Session 4: Staying Current Without Overwhelm**
How to keep learning without feeling drowned by the pace of change. Building a sustainable practice.
