You have spent decades building knowledge — about your profession, your community, your craft. That knowledge has enormous value. AI can help you share it more broadly, more effectively, without requiring you to be in a hundred places at once.

## Who This Is For

Women with professional, communal, or personal expertise who want to mentor, teach, or advise — formally or informally. AI experience helpful but not required.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: Identifying What You Have to Share**
Your expertise is so familiar to you that you may not recognize its value. We will map what you know and who needs to learn it.

**Session 2: AI as a Mentoring Amplifier**
Using AI to create resources, prepare sessions, answer common questions, and scale your impact — without losing the personal connection that makes mentoring work.

**Session 3: Building Your Mentoring Practice**
A concrete plan. Whether it is one-on-one, small groups, or content creation. Your knowledge, amplified. Your impact, multiplied.
