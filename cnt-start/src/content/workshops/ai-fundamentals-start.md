You have heard about AI. You have watched friends use it. You have wondered if you should learn it. This is where you start.

## Who This Is For

Women 18-24 who have never used AI or tried once and felt confused. You need a laptop and a willingness to try. Nothing else.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: What AI Actually Is**
Plain English. No jargon. What ChatGPT and similar tools are, how they work in simple terms, and why they matter for your future.

**Session 2: Your First Conversations With AI**
Prompting is not programming. It is asking clear questions. You will learn to talk to AI and get useful responses. By the end, you will have used AI to help with something real.

**Session 3: What AI Can and Cannot Do**
AI is powerful. It also makes things up. You will learn to tell the difference — when to trust the output, when to verify, when to walk away.

**Session 4: AI in Your Daily Life**
Schoolwork, job applications, personal organization, learning new topics. You will leave with a list of ways AI can serve your actual life right now.
