The transition from school or seminary to professional life is disorienting. Nobody teaches you how to write a resume that gets noticed, how to prepare for an interview, or how to present yourself. This workshop fills that gap — with AI as your partner.

## Who This Is For

Women 18-24 entering the workforce, applying to college or graduate programs, or looking for their first professional role. No experience needed.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: Resume and Cover Letter Writing With AI**
Build a professional resume from scratch or improve what you have. AI helps with phrasing, formatting, and tailoring for specific roles.

**Session 2: Job Search Strategy**
How to find opportunities, research companies, and identify roles that fit. AI as a research assistant.

**Session 3: Interview Preparation**
Common questions and how to answer them. Using AI for mock interviews and confidence building.

**Session 4: Professional Communication**
Email etiquette, LinkedIn presence, networking messages. Writing that sounds like you.

**Session 5: Building Your Online Presence**
What employers see when they search for you. How to shape that with AI assistance.

**Session 6: Your 90-Day Career Plan**
Concrete next steps. A timeline that respects where you are and where you want to go.
