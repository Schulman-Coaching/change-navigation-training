Career change has always been difficult. AI has made it both more urgent and more possible. This workshop helps you navigate the intersection — with clarity about what is changing, confidence in what you bring, and a concrete plan for what comes next.

## Who This Is For

Women who are returning to the workforce, considering a career shift, building a business, or simply wanting to understand how AI is reshaping professional opportunities. Some professional experience is helpful. AI experience is not required — we will build that together.

## What You Will Learn

**Session 1: The Changing Landscape**
What AI is actually doing to jobs — not the headlines, the data. Which roles are changing. Which skills are gaining value. How to think about your own position honestly and productively.

**Session 2: Your Transferable Skills**
The skills that matter most in an AI-augmented workplace are not technical. Judgment. Communication. Relationship-building. Adaptability. You will identify yours and learn to articulate them.

**Session 3: AI Skills That Open Doors**
Practical AI competencies that make a difference in hiring and advancement. You will build a portfolio of demonstrable skills — with specific tools, in your specific context.

**Session 4: Positioning and Communication**
How to talk about your experience, your AI literacy, and your value — in resumes, interviews, and professional conversations. Writing that reflects who you are and where you are going.

**Session 5: Building Your Path**
Exploring options — salaried roles, freelance, entrepreneurship, portfolio careers. Evaluating what fits your life, your values, and your goals. Not what you "should" do. What you want to do.

**Session 6: Your 90-Day Plan**
A concrete, realistic plan for the next three months. Specific actions. Support structures. Milestones. You will leave with clarity about the next step — and the one after that.
